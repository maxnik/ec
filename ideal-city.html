What Would Your Ideal City Look Like?

1. Have you ever lived in, or visited, a town or city that felt just right to you? If so, what was it that appealed to you, and why?

2. Imagine your ideal town or city. What would it look like? What would housing, commerce, transportation and recreation be like? What amenities or special features would it include, and why?

3. Do you agree that today’s cities are “the equivalent of the flip phone,” and are out of date? If so, how could they be redesigned and improved, and why?

4. What suggestions or ideas would you give to a city planner, if you could, and why?
