Can You Spot a Liar?

1. How good are you at detecting someone else’s lie? Have you heard about the ways professionals can spot a lie when they see one?

2. Do you know any ways — body language, for example — that are supposed to help you know when another person is lying?

Poker players might call it the “tell” — it’s the idea that your body language tells your questioners if the words you’re saying are actually true or false.

Take the quiz, then tell us:

3. How did you do on the quiz? Did you feel confident in detecting the truthtellers from the liars? Why?

4. What did you learn about yourself when taking the quiz, if anything, and why?

5. How difficult or easy is it to detect a lie? Why did you answer the way you did?
