What Do You Collect?

1. Do you consider yourself a collector? If so, what do you collect?

2. Is it a hobby to create, and tend to, a collection of things?

3. What objects are you aware of that people collect? Why do you think some people are drawn to collect them?

4. If you don’t collect anything, what, if anything, would you like to collect?

5. Why do you think clubs made up of collectors exist? Would you join a club in which all the members share an interest in the same thing you collect? Why or why not?

6. What do collections say about the person who amasses them? Why?
