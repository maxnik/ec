Do You Have Any Unlikely Friendships?

Who are your best friends? Are they mostly similar to you in terms of age and background, or do you have friends who are significantly different from you?

Do you think it is possible for two people with very different backgrounds to be friends? Why or why not? If so, what do you think it takes to build a solid relationship across differences?

Do you have any unlikely friendships? How did you meet and become friends? How have you kept up your friendship over time?

What do you think you can learn from being friends with someone who is different than you?
